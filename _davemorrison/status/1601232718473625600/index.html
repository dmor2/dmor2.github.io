Development of the original R-37 began in the early 1980s. A number of trials took place around 1994. One involved 'careful trajectory shaping', achieving a range in excess of 250km. The programme was discontinued in 1998. 2/7

Development of what would become the R-37M began around 2006. The seeker, propulsion and warhead differ significantly from the R-37. Janes reports that the manufacturer, Vympel, claimed performance was similar to the R-37. Janes does not provide the range achieved in trials. 3/7
