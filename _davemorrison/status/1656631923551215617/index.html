Past events.

In late 2022 or early 2023, aircraft of 18 ShAP and 266 ShAP overpainted many of their existing 'V' group markings and reapplied 'boxed V' markings ahead of the engine nacelles. This change is observed on aircraft deployed to Kursk-Khalino, Yeysk, Taganrog.
